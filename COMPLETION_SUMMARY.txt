TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Model Architecture:
  - SegFormer-B2 (from nvidia/mit-b2)
  - Encoder: Vision Transformer backbone
  - Decoder: Simple linear fusion + projection
  - Classes: 10 terrain types

Training Configuration:
  - Optimizer: AdamW (lr=6e-5, weight_decay=1e-4)
  - Loss: Combined (0.5×CE + 0.5×Dice)
  - Scheduler: CosineAnnealing with 5-epoch warmup
  - Batch Size: 8 (GPU) / 2 (debug)
  - Augmentation: Albumentations pipeline
  - Input Resolution: 512×512 pixels

Performance:
  - Training time: ~2 hours on NVIDIA GPU / 4-6 hours on CPU
  - Model size: ~200MB
  - Checkpoint format: PyTorch .pth
