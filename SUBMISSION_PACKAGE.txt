🎯 KEY SPECIFICATIONS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Model: SegFormer-B2 (HuggingFace)
Classes: 10 terrain types
Input: 512×512 pixels
Output: Per-pixel class predictions
Loss: 0.5×CrossEntropy (weighted) + 0.5×DiceLoss
Optimizer: AdamW (lr=6e-5)
Epochs: 20 full / 2 debug
Batch Size: 8 (GPU) / 2 (debug)
Augmentation: Albumentations (flip, noise, blur, color)
Scheduler: CosineAnnealing with 5-epoch warmup
